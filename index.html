Trimbakeshwar Jyotirling Temple
Trimbakeshwar Temple in Nashik is one of the twelve Jyotirlingas dedicated to Lord Shiva, known for its unique architecture and the sacred origin of the Godavari River.
Navsha Ganpati
Nashik's Nansha Ganpati is a revered temple dedicated to Lord Ganesha, known for its peaceful surroundings and deep-rooted local devotion. It is a popular spot for both spiritual seekers and tourists.
Kalaram Temple
Kalaram Mandir is an ancient temple dedicated to Lord Rama, known for its striking black stone architecture and black idols of Rama, Sita, and Lakshman. It was built in 1782 by Sardar Rangrao Odhekar.
Ganga Ghat
Ganga Ghat is a revered site along the Godavari River, known for its serene atmosphere and spiritual significance. It hosts rituals like bathing and offerings, attracting pilgrims and visitors seeking tranquility and cultural experiences.
Vallonne Vineyards
Vallonn√© Vineyards is India's first boutique winery, established in 2009, offering premium French-style wines, a vineyard resort, and a lake-facing restaurant serving Southeast Asian cuisine.
Pandav Leni Caves
Pandav Leni is a group of ancient Buddhist rock-cut caves carved between the 1st century BCE and 6th century CE, known for their intricate carvings and use as monasteries and prayer halls for monks.
Dhodap Fort
Chandwad Fort, built around 801 AD by the Yadava dynasty, is a historic hill fort that once guarded a key trade route and witnessed rule by several dynasties.
Kalmandavi Waterfall
Kalmandavi Waterfall is a 100-meter-deep, multi-tiered cascade located about 8 km south of Jawhar, surrounded by rugged terrain ideal for trekking and adventure activities. Flowing year-round, it's especially popular in summer for cliff jumping and nature exploration.
